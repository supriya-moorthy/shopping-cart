Our Menu

Ice cream comes in a wide variety of flavors, each offering a unique taste experience. Here's a look at some popular and creative ice cream flavors: Vanilla – A timeless classic, vanilla ice cream is creamy and sweet with a smooth, rich flavor. It's often the base for many sundaes and toppings, making it versatile and widely loved. Chocolate – Deep, rich, and indulgent, chocolate ice cream is a favorite among chocolate lovers. Made from cocoa or chocolate, it offers a luscious, velvety texture and a satisfying sweetness. Strawberry – Made with fresh strawberries or strawberry syrup, this flavor has a fruity, tangy sweetness that balances the creaminess of the ice cream. It's especially popular in summer. Mint Chocolate Chip – A refreshing mint base with chunks of chocolate throughout, this flavor combines cool mint with the crunch and richness of chocolate for a satisfying contrast. Cookies and Cream – Blending creamy vanilla ice cream with crushed chocolate sandwich cookies, this flavor is both crunchy and smooth, offering a delightful mix of textures. Salted Caramel – This flavor balances sweetness and saltiness, with a rich caramel swirl that melts into the creamy ice cream. It's perfect for those who love complex, contrasting flavors. Pistachio – Made with real pistachio nuts, this flavor has a subtle, nutty taste that’s slightly sweet and a bit savory, creating a creamy and crunchy experience.

About Us

Our Story

Welcome to chill spot, where our passion for ice cream meets the joy of creating delightful, handcrafted treats. Established in 1999, our shop was born from a simple love of high-quality ingredients and innovative flavors.

Our Mission

At chill spot, our mission is to deliver joy through every scoop of our ice cream. We strive to use the freshest local ingredients and create unique flavors that bring smiles to our customers' faces. Our commitment to quality and creativity is at the heart of everything we do.

Our Values

  • Quality: We use only the finest ingredients to ensure every scoop is perfect.
  • Creativity: Our flavor creations are inspired by traditional and modern tastes, always pushing boundaries.
  • Community: We believe in supporting local farmers and engaging with our community.
  • Sustainability: We are dedicated to environmentally friendly practices in all aspects of our business.
